Now, onto assembling our sandwich!

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam of your choosing
  • 2 slice of your chosen bread
  • Knife

Steps:

  1. Firstly, you are going to want to lay out your pieces of bread.

  2. With your knife, spread butter and peanut butter on the right hand slice.

  3. Now after washing your knife, spread the jam on your left hamd slice.

  4. Lastly put the two slices together and enjoy your freshly made PB&J!
